## INDICATIONS

- Gout
- Uric acid diathesis
- Hyperuricaemia

---

## PRINCIPLES

- Limit purines (moderate fish/meat)
- Fluid: 2–2.5L/day
- Alkaline urine: more beans/vegetables
- 5 meals/day; salt ≤8g/day
